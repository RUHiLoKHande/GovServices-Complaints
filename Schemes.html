Schemes for Farmers

Farmers' schemes promote sustainability, boost yields and income, securing farmers' lives and driving economic progress.

Shravan Bal Yojana 2023 Maharashtra Information

It provides support to eligible beneficiaries. For documents, scheme lists, beneficiary lists, and online forms, please check the official Maharashtra government website for complete details in Marathi.

Mahasamriddhi Women Empowerment Scheme

" It aims to empower women in the state. For specific details and updates, please refer to the Maharashtra government's official website or relevant authorities.

Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Farmer Debt Relief Scheme 2023 Marathi

"Maharashtra Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Loan Waiver Scheme 2023" and related lists, consult the official Maharashtra government website in Marathi.

Mahajobs Portal 2023 Maharashtra Information Marathi

For online registration and applications on the "Mahajobs Portal 2023" by the Maharashtra government, visit mahajobs.maharashtra.gov.in.

Self Balasaheb Thackeray Road Accident Insurance Scheme

For information on the "Self Balasaheb Thackeray Accident Insurance Scheme 2022" in Maharashtra and online registration, visit the official government portal for details in Marathi.

Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Subsistence Allowance Scheme

To apply for the "Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Scholarship 2023" and the "Hostel Maintenance Allowance" in Maharashtra, visit the MahaDBT portal for online applications.

Rajmata Jijau Maternal and Child Health and Nutrition Mission 2023

"Rajmata Jijau Mother Child Health & Nutrition Mission" in Maharashtra, please refer to the details available in Marathi.

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

In July 2020, the Union Cabinet approved the Agriculture Infrastructure Fund, providing debt financing for post-harvest and community farming projects.
